ChillGym Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 May 2026

ChillGym is a personal gym-tracking app. This page explains what data the app handles, where it lives, and what we share. The short version: nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

1. What ChillGym stores

The app saves the following on the device you install it on:

  • Workout sessions, sets, templates, custom exercises, and your preferences.
  • Bodyweight entries you log.
  • Photos you choose to attach to exercises or body-progress entries.
  • Workout-share images that you save or share through the system share sheet.

2. Where it is stored

On iOS and Android, data is kept in the app's private storage area (UserDefaults / SharedPreferences for settings, and the app's Documents directory for photos). On the web build, data is kept in your browser's local storage. In all cases the data stays on the device or browser profile where you created it.

3. What ChillGym does not do

  • We do not run any servers that store your data.
  • We do not have user accounts or sign-in.
  • We do not use analytics, ad networks, or trackers.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not access your phone's photo library except when you tap "Take photo" or "Pick from gallery", and only the photo you select is copied into the app.

4. Permissions the app may ask for

  • Camera — only when you tap a button that captures a photo.
  • Photo library — only when you pick or save a photo.
  • Notifications — only used for the rest timer and inactivity nudges. You can deny or revoke this at any time in system settings.

5. Export and deletion

You can export all of your data as a JSON file from Profile → Data → Export, and import it back on the same or a different device. You can also clear everything from Profile → Data → Delete all data; this wipes the app's stored sessions, templates, custom exercises, bodyweight entries, and preferences. Uninstalling the app removes everything as well.

6. Children

ChillGym is not directed at children under 13. If you are under the age your jurisdiction defines for digital consent, please do not use the app without a parent or guardian.

7. Changes to this policy

If we change anything material on this page we will update the date at the top. There is no automated mechanism to notify you because we hold no contact details.
